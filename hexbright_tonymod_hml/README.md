hexbright_tonymod_hml
===========================

 Instructions:
 From off:
 
 Short tap for:              Long press for:
 High                        Dazzle
 Short tap for:              Short tap for:
 Medium                      Fast blinking
 Short tap for:              Short tap for:
 Low                         Slow blinking
 Short tap for:              Short tap for:
 Off                         Off
 
 A long press while any mode is activated will shut the HexBright off.
